Our Services


Chatbot

A simple chatbot to help even the illiterate to use the site.


Feedback System

Real time communication and feedback between refugees and refugee camps.


Survey System

An easier way for refugees to get the latest surveys and respond to them.


News and Community posts

Making it easier for camps and refugees to get information on what's happening within the camp and other camps. Camps can share ideas here.
